Your Wishlist

Adding Items

  1. Click "My List" in the navigation bar
  2. Click the "Add Item" button
  3. Fill out the item information:
    • Item Title (required) — Name of the item
    • Description — Be specific! Include size, color, model number, or any preferences
      Example: "Size Medium, Navy Blue, Model XR-500"
    • Link/URL — Where to buy it (Amazon, store website, etc.)
    • Price — Helps gift-givers budget
    • Allow multiple people to buy this — Check this for items like gift cards
  4. Click "Save Item"
💡 Tip Click the ℹ️ icons next to field labels for helpful tips!

Editing Items

  1. Go to "My List"
  2. Find the item you want to edit
  3. Click the pencil icon (✏️)
  4. Make your changes
  5. Click "Save Item"
What if someone already claimed my item?

You can still edit it! The person who claimed it won't be notified of the changes, so major changes (like completely different item) might confuse them. Small updates (like adding color preference) are fine.

Organizing Your List (Priority Order)

Items at the top of your list are most wanted. Use the up/down arrows to reorder:

  1. Go to "My List"
  2. Click the up arrow (↑) to move an item higher in priority
  3. Click the down arrow (↓) to move an item lower in priority
Why this matters Family members see your items in this order, so they know what you want most!

Deleting Items

  1. Go to "My List"
  2. Find the item you want to delete
  3. Click the trash icon (🗑️)
  4. Confirm deletion
⚠️ Important If someone claimed this item, they'll be notified that you deleted it. This can't be undone!

Viewing Your List

Your "My List" page has two tabs:

  • Active Items — Your current wishlist (what you want now)
  • Received Items — Archive of gifts you've received

What you can see:

  • ✅ All your items with descriptions, links, and prices
  • ✅ The order of your items (priority)
  • ❌ What's been claimed from your list (keeps the surprise!)
  • ❌ Who claimed anything (total privacy)

Received Items (Archive)

Why Use the Archive?

After birthdays and holidays, you'll receive gifts from your wishlist. Marking them as "received" keeps your active wishlist clean and current while maintaining a history of what you've gotten.

Marking Items as Received

  1. Go to "My List"
  2. Make sure you're on the "Active Items" tab
  3. Find the item you received
  4. Click the green checkmark button (✓ Got It!)
  5. The item moves to your "Received Items" tab
When to do this After Christmas, birthdays, or any time you receive a gift from your list!

Viewing Received Items

  1. Go to "My List"
  2. Click the "Received Items" tab
  3. You'll see all items you've marked as received with the date

Restoring Items to Active List

Want the same item again (different size, color, or just want another)? You can restore it:

  1. Go to "My List" → "Received Items" tab
  2. Find the item you want again
  3. Click "Restore"
  4. Update any details (description, URL, price, etc.)
  5. Click "Save Item"
  6. A NEW COPY appears on your Active Items list
Example You got a blue sweater (size M) for Christmas. Mark it received. Next year you want another in size L. Restore the item, change the description to "size L, navy color," save. Now you have a new active item and the history of what you got last year!

Managing Children's Lists

When to Create a Child Profile

Create a child profile only for young children who can't manage their own wishlist yet (typically under 10-12 years old).

⚠️ Important Warnings
  • Coordinate with your spouse! Only ONE parent should create the child's profile
  • Don't create for older children/adults — They should have their own account
  • Check first! Your spouse may have already created it

Creating a Child Profile

  1. Go to "My List"
  2. Scroll to the bottom
  3. Click "Add Child Profile"
  4. Enter your child's name
  5. Click "Add Child"

Managing Your Child's List

Once created, you'll see your child's list in the "Children's Lists You Manage" section.

  1. Go to "My List"
  2. Scroll to "Children's Lists You Manage"
  3. Click on your child's name
  4. Add, edit, organize, and manage items just like your own list

What About the Other Parent?

Both parents can view the child's list when shopping for family, but only the parent who created it can manage (add/edit/delete items).

Shopping for Others

Finding Family Members' Lists

  1. Click "Dashboard" in the navigation bar
  2. You'll see cards for each family member
  3. Click "Shop Their Wishlist" on anyone's card

Claiming Items

When you decide to buy something, claim it so others know it's covered:

  1. Browse a family member's wishlist
  2. Find an item you want to buy
  3. Click the "I'll Get This!" button
  4. The item shows as "Claimed" to other shoppers
Privacy Protected The recipient CANNOT see that you claimed their item. It's still a surprise!

Unclaiming Items

Changed your mind? No problem:

  1. Go back to the person's wishlist
  2. Find the item you claimed
  3. Click "Changed My Mind"
  4. The item becomes available for others again

Filtering Available Items

Toggle "Show only available items" to hide claimed items and focus on what's still needed.

Account Settings

Updating Your Profile

  1. Click your name in the top-right corner
  2. Select "Profile"
  3. Update your display name or gift delivery email
  4. Click "Save Changes"

Gift Delivery Email

This is where digital gifts (e-gift cards, online orders) should be sent. It defaults to your account email but you can change it to a different address.

Changing Your Password

  1. Go to Profile
  2. Click "Change Password"
  3. Enter your current password
  4. Enter your new password twice
  5. Click "Change Password"

Tips & Best Practices

For Better Wishlists

  • Be specific — Include size, color, model, etc.
  • Add links — Makes it easy for gift-givers
  • Include prices — Helps people budget
  • Prioritize — Put your most wanted items at the top
  • Keep it updated — Remove items you no longer want
  • Mark received items — Keep your list clean after holidays

For Better Gift-Giving

  • Claim items early — Prevents duplicates
  • Check priority — Top items are most wanted
  • Unclaim if plans change — Let others know it's available
  • Check closer to the holiday — Lists may have been updated
