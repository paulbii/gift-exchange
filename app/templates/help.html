What is Power Family Wishlist?

Power Family Wishlist is a private system for our family to share gift ideas and coordinate gift-giving. No more guessing what people want, no more duplicate gifts, and everyone still gets surprised!

How It Works:

  1. You create a wishlist - Add items you'd like to receive with links, descriptions, and prices
  2. Family can browse your list - They see what you want and can claim items
  3. Privacy is protected - You can't see what's been claimed from YOUR list, so gifts are still a surprise!
  4. No duplicates - When someone claims an item, others know it's covered

Getting Started

Step 1: Request an Invitation

Text your email address to Paul (the family admin).

Example: "My email is sarah@example.com"

Step 2: Receive Your Invitation

You'll get your invitation in two ways:

  • Email invitation - Check your inbox (and spam folder!)
  • Text message with link - From Paul as a backup

Use either method - they both work!

Step 3: Create Your Account

  1. Click the link from either the email or text message
  2. You'll see a welcome screen with your name
  3. Choose a password and enter it twice
  4. Click "Complete Setup"
‚úì You're in! You'll be automatically logged in and can start adding items to your wishlist.

Tips:

  • Keep both the email and text in case you need the link again
  • Choose a password you'll remember
  • Write it down somewhere safe if needed
What if I don't receive anything?
  • Check your spam/junk folder for the email
  • Make sure you gave Paul the correct email address
  • Text Paul to resend the invitation link

Quick Feature Overview

Managing Your Wishlist

  • Add items with descriptions, links, and prices
  • Organize by priority (most wanted at top)
  • Edit or delete items anytime
  • Mark items as "received" after holidays to keep your list clean

Shopping for Family

  • Browse everyone's wishlists from the dashboard
  • Claim items to let others know you've got it covered
  • Unclaim if you change your mind
  • Filter to see only available items

Children's Lists (For Parents)

  • Create and manage wishlists for young children
  • One parent creates the list, both can view it
  • When kids are old enough, they get their own account

Archive (Received Items)

  • Mark items as "received" after getting them as gifts
  • Keep a history of past gifts
  • Restore items to your active list if you want them again
